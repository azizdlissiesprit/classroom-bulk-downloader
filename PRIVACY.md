# Privacy Policy — Bulk Downloader for Google Classroom

_Last updated: 2026-06-26_

This privacy policy explains how the "Bulk Downloader for Google Classroom" browser
extension ("the extension") handles information. In short: **the extension does not
collect, store, sell, or transmit any of your data. Everything happens locally in your
own browser.**

## What the extension does

When you click the extension's toolbar icon on a Google Classroom or Google Drive page,
the extension reads the **currently open page** to find attached files (their names and
file identifiers). It then shows you a list so you can choose which files to download.
When you click "Download", the selected files are saved to your browser's Downloads folder.

## Data the extension accesses

- **Page content (file names and file IDs):** Read only from the tab you are actively
  viewing, and only at the moment you click the extension icon. This information is used
  solely to build the on-screen list of files and to construct download links. It is held
  in memory only while the popup is open and is never sent anywhere.
- **Your Google session:** Downloads are performed by your browser using your existing
  Google login (cookies), exactly as if you had clicked the file yourself. The extension
  never sees, stores, or transmits your password or login tokens.

## Data the extension does NOT do

- It does **not** collect or store personal information.
- It does **not** send any data to the developer or to any third-party server.
- It does **not** use analytics, tracking, or advertising.
- It does **not** load or run any remote code.
- It does **not** access tabs or pages other than the one you explicitly run it on.

## Permissions and why they are needed

- **activeTab / scripting:** to read the file links on the page you are viewing, only when
  you click the icon.
- **downloads:** to save the files you choose into your Downloads folder.
- **Host access to Google domains (classroom.google.com, drive.google.com,
  docs.google.com, drive.usercontent.google.com):** to detect attachments and download the
  selected files from Google's servers.
